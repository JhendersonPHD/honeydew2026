# HoneyDew 3D Printed Products Research Report

**Research Date:** April 16, 2026  
**Purpose:** Identify 3D printed product opportunities for HoneyDew farm-to-consumer Shopify store

---

## Executive Summary

Adding 3D printed merchandise to HoneyDew presents a low-risk, high-margin opportunity to diversify revenue beyond fresh produce. The print-on-demand (POD) model requires no inventory investment, integrates seamlessly with Shopify, and can reinforce HoneyDew's brand identity around farm-fresh, sustainable living.

## 4. Supplier Options

### Print-on-Demand Services Comparison

| Supplier | 3D Products | Shopify Integration | Base Location | Key Strength |
|----------|-------------|--------------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Printful** | Limited (mostly 2D) | Native | USA | Premium quality, no base fee |
| **Printify** | Moderate | Native | Global network | Lowest prices, broad catalog |
| **Print Aura** | Limited | Native | USA | Good for apparel |
| **Gooten** | Moderate | Native | Global network | Book publishing, home decor |
| **Teelaunch** | Limited | Native | Global | Budget option |
| **SPOKE** | Moderate | Native | UK/EU | Eco-friendly focus |

### Specialized 3D POD Services

1. **Shapeways** (Etsy, own store)
   - True 3D printed products
   - Wide material options (plastic, metal, resin)
   - Higher prices but unique products
   - Integration: Etsy, own website

2. **DIY 3D Printing**
   - Purchase own 3D printer (Creality Ender 3: ~$200)
   - Print products in-house
   - Higher margins, time investment
   - Best for: Small batches, custom requests

3. **Fusion Box / 3D Printed Sunglasses**
   - Niche eyewear
   - Custom designs

### Recommended Approach for HoneyDew

**Phase 1 (Months 1-3):** Use Printify for apparel and accessories
- Lowest risk, fastest setup
- Focus on stickers, t-shirts, tote bags, mugs
- Test which designs sell best

**Phase 2 (Months 4-6):** Add Printful for premium apparel
- Better quality for best-sellers
- Expand to hoodies, premium tees

**Phase 3 (Months 6-12):** Explore Shapeways for unique 3D products
- Custom planters, kitchen items
- Higher margins, longer production time acceptable

## 6. Pricing Strategy

### Cost-Plus Pricing Model

```
Sell Price = (Cost of Goods + Base Margin) / (1 - Payment Processing Fee - Platform Fee)
```

### Example Calculations

**Custom T-Shirt**
- POD Cost: $10.00
- Desired Margin: 40%
- Platform Fee (Shopify): 2.9% + $0.30
- Payment Processing: 2.6%
- Calculation: ($10 + $4) / (1 - 0.029 - 0.026) = $14.65
- **Recommended Price: $24.99**

**Custom Mug**
- POD Cost: $7.00
- Desired Margin: 45%
- Calculation: ($7 + $3.15) / (1 - 0.055) = $10.69
- **Recommended Price: $22.99**

**Tote Bag**
- POD Cost: $5.00
- Desired Margin: 50%
- Calculation: ($5 + $2.50) / (1 - 0.055) = $7.93
- **Recommended Price: $19.99**

### Competitive Pricing Bands
- **Budget:** $4.99-14.99 (stickers, small accessories)
- **Mid:** $19.99-34.99 (apparel, standard mugs)
- **Premium:** $39.99-65.99 (hoodies, bundles)
- **Luxury:** $79.99+ (custom art, gift sets)
