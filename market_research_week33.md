1. **Subscription model** — CRITICAL. Competitors all have this. Without it, no recurring revenue.
2. **Farm review/rating system** — Social proof drives conversion. Good Eggs 4.6 avg rating.
3. **Recipe integration** — Content marketing, increases AOV by estimated 25-30%
4. **Order tracking page** — Customer expectation since Instacart set the bar
5. **Stripe payment completion** — Current checkout non-functional for real payments
